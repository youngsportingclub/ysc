Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

Promote sports and healthy lifestyles among youth
Encourage youth participation in community activities
Develop teamwork and leadership among young people
Organize sporting events and tournaments
Build a strong and united community
🔭

Our Vision

Become the most respected community sports club in the region
Empower youth through sports, unity and social responsibility
Create opportunities for young talent to flourish
Inspire the next generation of community leaders
Maintain a legacy of excellence since 2009
Our Objectives

Goals & Objectives

The core objectives that guide Young Sporting Club towards its mission.

🤝
Fostering Unity & Harmony
Maintain mutual cooperation, sympathy, friendship and harmony among the youth of West Shingrauli and greater Shingrauli area, and promote their physical and mental development through sports.
⭐
Upholding Club Prestige
Uphold the dignity of this traditional club by fulfilling duties and responsibilities with honesty, efficiency and dedication, and inspire all members to work for the welfare of the country and nation.
🆘
Member Support
Take necessary steps to resolve any problems faced by club members.
🏆
Sports Development
Organize seasonal Premier League, tournaments etc. to develop club members in the sports arena and promote them through appropriate channels. Arrange all necessary materials for this purpose.
Our Journey

Club History

From humble beginnings in West Shingrauli to a proud sporting legacy in Moulvibazar.

2009
The Foundation — 13 January 2009
Young Sporting Club was founded on Monday, 13 January 2009 by a group of enthusiastic young people in West Shingrauli, Shamshernagar, Kamalganj, Moulvibazar.
2010
First Football Tournament
The club organized its first official football tournament, establishing YSC as a serious sporting organization.
2012
Cricket Wing Established
A dedicated cricket team was formed, expanding the club beyond football and attracting new talent.
2015
Community Recognition
YSC received recognition as an active youth organization. Badminton events were also introduced.
2018
Cultural Wing & Social Programs
The club expanded to include annual cultural programs, Eid celebrations and community service initiatives.
2026
17 Years Strong — Still Going!
Young Sporting Club celebrates 17+ years with 100+ active members, continuing to inspire the next generation.

Club Structure

President

Leads the club, oversees all operations and represents Young Sporting Club.

General Secretary
