Our Journey

Club History

From humble beginnings in Shingrahully to a proud sporting legacy in Moulvibazar.

2009
The Foundation — 13 January 2009
Young Sporting Club was founded on Monday, 13 January 2009 by a group of enthusiastic young people in Shingrahully, Shamsher Nagar, Kamalganj, Moulvibazar.
2010
First Football Tournament
The club organized its first official football tournament, establishing YSC as a serious sporting organization.
2012
Cricket Wing Established
A dedicated cricket team was formed, expanding the club beyond football and attracting new talent.
2015
Community Recognition
YSC received recognition as an active youth organization. Badminton events were also introduced.
2018
Cultural Wing & Social Programs
The club expanded to include annual cultural programs, Eid celebrations and community service initiatives.
2026
17 Years Strong — Still Going!
Young Sporting Club celebrates 17+ years with 100+ active members, continuing to inspire the next generation.

Club Structure

🏅

President

Leads the club, oversees all operations and represents Young Sporting Club.

🥈

Vice President

Supports the President and leads club activities in their absence.

📋

General Secretary

Manages club records, communications and coordinates all club programs.

💰

Treasurer

Manages the club's finances, maintains accounts and oversees all transactions.

⚽

Sports Secretary

Organizes and manages all sports events, tournaments and training programs.
