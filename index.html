Who We Are

More Than Just A
Sports Club

Young Sporting Club was founded on 13 January 2009 by enthusiastic young people in Shingrahully, Shamsher Nagar, Kamalganj, Moulvibazar.

For over 17 years, the club has been a home for young athletes building teamwork, character and community spirit through sports and social activities.
